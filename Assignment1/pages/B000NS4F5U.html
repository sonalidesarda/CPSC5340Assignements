With the help of a princess, Siep embarks on a daring mission to rescue the king and annihilate his adversaries. THE LEGENDARY WARRIOR is a riveting tale of bravery and heroism...... Djamel Arbouche
5.0 out of 5 starsEntertaining drama on a legendary period
March 26, 2009
Format: DVD
"The Legendary Warrior" was the first chinese TV drama I watched. At the time, I was looking for some good action and history drama, in standard mandarin, but with English subtitles. I purchased that after watching a small preview on the web. I was not disappointed. This epic series tells the story of Xue Ren Gui, a skilled fighter who rose from low-rank status to become the Tang emperor's best general.
The series is addictive, with a superb pilot episode showing what makes this show so good: a mythical context, superb fight scenes, good music. I was hooked right from the start.
The english subtitles are good. They do have some flaws, such as a few spelling mistakes, or the use of familiar english at places, but they really allow you to follow the story.
I have to mention that disc 13 has a wonderful fight scene, which is quite long, between Luo Tong and the prince. I was impressed by this scene, which is of outstanding level for a TV series.
Other than that, the picutre quality is very good, as is the sound. I hope to find other series like this one!
