I have gradually worked up to Level 3 for my Aeropilates Reformer and I must say that Pilates is a wonderful workout for those of us with trouble zones that nothing else seems to get rid of. Its an amazing full body workout from your head to your toes, and the famous quote "Doing Pilates twice a week will maintain your body, Three times a week will change it" is true. I workout 5 times a week for 30-45 minutes solely with the Stamina Aeropilates Cardio and Integrated DVDs and I have lost over 50 pounds and feel amazing. I started off with level1 and slowly graduated to the third level. You could easily just do one of the tapes and feel the amazing results after a few workouts. Try it, you'll feel great and look great too!

One helpful tip that I learned in a studio Pilates class: proper breathing is essential to the exercise, so when you exhale, do a deep from the stomach "hhhaaaa" exhale. You may be a bit loud at times, but the results will speak for themselves.
