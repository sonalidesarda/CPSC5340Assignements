5.0 out of 5 starsUnique Prison Flick
July 22, 2018
Format: Prime VideoVerified Purchase
I have to say that I was not expecting a whole lot from this movie - just the standard prison flick. I got something entirely different.

If you can imagine somebody preaching the gospel non-ironically while filling the air with some of the choicest words in all of the English language, then you have conquered the first unique element of the film. Then factor in that ultimately, the main character, who clearly has the reputation for being something of a violent criminal, ends up as something of a Christ figure, and you are still only at number two on the list of things that make this a far different prison film.

This was definitely better than the average prison movie. It is well worth the watch. It is very engrossing and compelling, but there are so many plot twists and turns that watching it once will be all that you ever need to watch it. Frankly, I have a case of whiplash from all of the plot twists and reversals.
