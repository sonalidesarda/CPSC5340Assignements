Editorial Reviews

A Reggae-soaked noir film about a photographer who falls in love with a dance hall girl and her world of crime.

Stepped in the human atmosphere of Jamaica, and set against a classic, pulsating score, KLASH combines the hard-edged style of ''THE HARDER THEY COME'' with the sultry intrigue of film noir. A world-weary photographer past his prime comes to Kingston to photograph the Klash, an electrifying battle of the world's best Reggae and Dancehall performers.

From Peter Allen, writer of the 2010 hit action film 'Takers'

Stars Jasmine Guy (A Different World, Dead like Me) and Giancarlo Esposito (Breaking Bad, Better Call Saul, Do the Right Thing)
